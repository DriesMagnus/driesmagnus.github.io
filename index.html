About Me

Hi there! Thank you for checking out my portfolio!
My name is Dries Magnus, but I go by "Magnum". I am a 23 year old student and esports observer from Belgium.

"VALORANT really catapulted me into the world of esports."

"My perfectionistic nature makes me want to deliver the best viewing experience possible and be the best observer that I can be."

My competitive nature got me to play the VALORANT Closed Beta, as I tried to improve and compete at a collegiate level.

While doing so, I found a lot of passion and satisfaction in observing instead of competing. Thus, I started observing local tournaments, pushing myself to make the broadcast as enjoyable to watch as possible.

I have also been interviewed by EsportsDriven, which you can find here.

I would love to work with you!
You can check out my work or contact me here.
